underground

This story is about a human child who fell into the realm of monsters

loosely based off of "Undertale" which belongs to Toby Fox.





There was once a human who fell in a hole on the side of a mountain. the mountain was said to be cursed, and that anyone who climbed it would dissapear forever. when the human hear this, they wanted to see for themselves. after falling into the crevice, the human woke up on a flowerbed of buttercups. they had softened the landing. the human could see no way of climbing out of the crevice, but they saw something that looked like a path leading deeper into the cave. they followed it, and found what looked like city ruins.
